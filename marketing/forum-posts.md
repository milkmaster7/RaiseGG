### HLTV Post 9 — Technical/Educational
**Title:** how blockchain escrow actually works for CS2 stake matches
**Section:** CS2 General
**Suggested time:** Tuesday 15:00 CET

**Body:**
been seeing questions about how escrow works on stake platforms so here's a simple explanation.

1. both players deposit USDC into a solana smart contract before the match
2. the contract locks the funds — nobody can withdraw
3. match plays out on a CS2 server with MatchZy plugin
4. match result gets verified (steam API confirms the score)
5. smart contract releases all funds to the winner
6. if there's a dispute, the contract has a resolution mechanism

the key point: your money is NOT held by a company. it's in a smart contract on the blockchain. even the platform creators can't steal it.

this is how RaiseGG works. open source contract, verifiable on-chain. compare this to discord wagers where you're trusting a random admin.
