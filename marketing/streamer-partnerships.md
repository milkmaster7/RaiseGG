## Outreach Strategy

### Step 1: Gather Contact Info (Day 1)
For each streamer above, visit their Twitch channel and extract:
- Business email (usually in channel panels or About section)
- Discord server invite link
- Twitter/X handle
- Instagram handle

Most streamers list contact info in their Twitch panels below the stream. Twitch DMs require following the channel first.

### Step 2: Craft the Pitch

**Subject:** Partnership Opportunity - RaiseGG Competitive Gaming Platform

**Key talking points:**
- RaiseGG is a 1v1 competitive stake platform with crypto prizes
- Perfect fit for their competitive/ranked audience
- Revenue share on signups from their referral link
- Free platform credits for on-stream demonstrations
- Custom referral codes (e.g., SKIP_SG, TARGETLOCATED)

**What we offer streamers:**
1. **Referral commission** -- % of every deposit from referred users
2. **Free credits** -- $50-100 in platform credits for on-stream play
3. **Custom overlay/panels** -- Branded RaiseGG assets for their stream
4. **Featured streamer page** -- Listed on RaiseGG.gg as official partner
5. **Priority support** -- Direct Discord line to RaiseGG team

### Step 3: Outreach Priority Order
1. **Turkish CS2 streamers** (spyhiddentv first -- we have email)
2. **CIS Deadlock streamers** (Skip_SG, kirill_trix -- largest audiences)
3. **Balkan streamers** (petrowch0, mireoriginal)
4. **Stretch targets** as needed

### Step 4: Template DM for Twitch

```
Hey [NAME]! Been watching your [CS2/Deadlock/Dota] streams -- love the competitive content.

I'm reaching out from RaiseGG, a new platform for 1v1 competitive matches with crypto prizes. Think FACEIT but with real stakes.

We're looking for competitive streamers to partner with and thought your content would be a perfect fit. We offer:
- Referral revenue share on all signups
- Free platform credits for on-stream play
- Custom referral code

Would you be interested in chatting? Happy to jump on Discord or email.

Cheers!
```

## Key Market Insights from Research

- **Turkey:** CS2 is exploding after Aurora Gaming's 2025 EWC success. Turkish CS2 viewership is growing fast. 6 of our targets are in this market.
- **CIS/Russia:** Deadlock has massive CIS adoption -- Russian is the 2nd-largest language for Deadlock viewership. Dota 2 roots make CIS audiences natural fits for competitive stake gaming.
- **Balkans:** Smaller market but passionate gaming communities in Bulgaria and Serbia. CS2 is the dominant competitive title.
- **Deadlock timing:** The game is still early -- partnering now means being associated with the scene before it gets saturated with sponsors.
- **CIS streaming ecosystem:** Russian-speaking streamers frequently collaborate, cross-promote, and share audiences. Getting one CIS streamer can cascade to others.
