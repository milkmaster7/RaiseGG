# Product Hunt Launch — RaiseGG

## Tagline (60 chars max)
Play CS2 & Dota 2 1v1 for USDC with blockchain escrow

## Description
RaiseGG is a competitive gaming platform where players stake USDC on their own matches — 1v1 in CS2, Dota 2, and Deadlock. Both players deposit into a Solana smart contract before the match. Winner gets paid automatically. No trust needed.

**Key features:**
- 1v1, 2v2, and 5v5 stake matches ($1-$50 USDC)
- Solana blockchain escrow — funds locked until match ends
- Anti-cheat on all dedicated servers (VAC + MatchZy)
- Free daily tournaments — no deposit, $5 USDC prize
- City-based leaderboards (Istanbul vs Belgrade vs Bucharest)
- ELO ranking system across all three games
- Instant USDC payouts via Solana (~400ms)

**Why we built this:**
Wager gaming has always had a trust problem. Player A wins, Player B doesn't pay. We solved this with blockchain escrow — both deposits go into a smart contract, winner gets paid automatically. No middleman, no scams.

We target regions where traditional payment rails don't work well — Turkey, Balkans, CIS, Central Asia, Caucasus. USDC on Solana is the great equalizer.

**Tech stack:**
Next.js, Supabase (PostgreSQL), Solana (SPL Token escrow), dedicated game servers with anti-cheat.
