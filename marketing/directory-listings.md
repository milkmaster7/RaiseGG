## Standard Content (use across all directories)

**Platform Name:** RaiseGG

**URL:** https://raisegg.gg

**Tagline:** 1v1 Stake Matches in CS2, Dota 2 & Deadlock with Blockchain Escrow

**Short Description (50 words):**
RaiseGG is a competitive gaming platform where players stake USDC on 1v1, 2v2, and 5v5 matches in CS2, Dota 2, and Deadlock. All wagers are held in Solana smart contract escrow and auto-paid to winners. VAC-verified, ELO-ranked, with free daily tournaments and city-based leaderboards.

**Long Description (150 words):**
RaiseGG is a next-generation competitive gaming platform built for players who want to put real stakes behind their skill. Challenge opponents to 1v1, 2v2, or 5v5 matches in CS2, Dota 2, and Deadlock — with USDC wagers locked in Solana blockchain escrow before the match starts. No trust required, no chargebacks, no middleman. The smart contract releases funds to the verified winner automatically.

Every player is VAC-checked, ELO-ranked, and tied to their Steam profile. The platform runs free daily tournaments with real prize pools for players who want to compete without risking anything. City-based leaderboards, seasonal rankings, and bounty matches keep the competitive loop tight.

RaiseGG targets underserved regions — CIS, Turkey, Balkans, Central Asia — where competitive players lack reliable platforms for skill-based wagering. Built on Next.js with Solana smart contracts, it is fast, transparent, and available worldwide without KYC.
